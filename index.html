What is ProdigiConnect?

Taste, Play, Earn
Food on Blockchain!

ProdigiConnect is a blockchain-powered ecosystem designed to connect food lovers, restaurant owners, and travelers by integrating GameFi mechanics, real-world adoption (RWA), and tokenized incentives.

  • Explore local street food from around the world.
  • Complete mini-game food missions at restaurants.
  • Write food reviews and earn PDG tokens.

Creating a decentralized and interactive global food ecosystem.
